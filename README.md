<h2 align="center"><em><code>Dexarm</code></em></h2>

Dexarm is a low-cost, modular prosthetic arm designed to restore functional independence for individuals with upper-limb loss currently in development. The system uses precise motor control to enable natural movements such as gripping, lifting, and releasing objects, allowing users to perform everyday tasks with greater ease and confidence.

Feel free to check out our [website](https://vividsense.netlify.app) for more information about our company and products there!
